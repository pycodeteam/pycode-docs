# Kurel Facts

Kurel Facts is a project dedicated to structuring and preserving cultural data.

## Objective

- Document cultural performances
- Structure information related to content and context
- Create a searchable and structured database

## Data

- Performances
- Durations
- Audio references
- Locations
- Participants

## Approach

- Field data collection
- Documentation and structuring
- Progressive enrichment

## Vision

Kurel Facts aims to contribute to the digitalisation and preservation of cultural heritage through structured data.
