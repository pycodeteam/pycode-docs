# Lekkantu

Lekkantu is a data platform focused on nutrition and food products.

## Objective

- Structure food product data
- Improve access to nutritional information
- Support healthier consumption habits

## Data collected

- Category
- Brand
- Barcode
- Ingredients
- Allergens
- Nutritional values (energy, proteins, sugars, fats, salt, etc.)

## Approach

- Field data collection
- Standardisation of product information
- Centralisation in a structured database

## Vision

Lekkantu aims to become a reference database for food products, enabling:

- product comparison
- data analysis
- future applications (mobile, scoring, recommendations)
